Alicia Magro

Hello and welcome to my virtual resume ! My name is Alicia Magro and I’m 29 years old. I am a former nurse who used to work in psychiatric wards for adults. After a few years of working as a psychiatric nurse, I have decided to begin a professional retraining.


Since I was very young, I have always been passionate about the digital field so it seemed only natural to me to choose one of these professions for my career change. After a skills assessment and a lot of thoughts, I stared to get very interested in web development. To confirm my choice, I was lucky enough to do an immersion internship for a week, at a company located in Euratechnologies in Lille. This internship, which I really enjoyed, allowed me to discover a whole new field that before that, was completely unknown to me. Afterward, I followed the BTech program with the Z association, which allowed me to confirm my great interest in web development. To do so, I am currently looking for a training to learn more about web development in order to make it my profession.
